## 🔍 Detailed Player Profiles

### 1. Metricool (The Data Analyst)
*   **Website:** [metricool.com](https://metricool.com)
*   **Pricing (USD):**
    *   **Free ($0):** 1 brand, 20 posts/mo, basic analytics.
    *   **Starter ($25/mo or $20/mo annual):** Up to 10 brands, unlimited scheduling, LinkedIn connection, PDF reports.
    *   **Advanced ($67/mo or $53/mo annual):** Up to 50 brands, team management, post approval system, API access.
*   **The Vibe:** Best-in-class data visualization and ad management.
*   **Limitation:** Their media library is basic, making it hard to manage a large "vault" of clips.

### 2. Publer (The Utility Knife)
*   **Website:** [publer.io](https://publer.io)
*   **Pricing (PLN - Base 1 Account):**
    *   **Free (0 PLN):** 3 accounts (except X), 10 scheduled posts/account, 25 saved drafts.
    *   **Professional (18.25 PLN/mo or 14.60 PLN annual):** Unlimited posts, 1st comment scheduling, Twitter/X integration, trending news.
    *   **Business (36.50 PLN/mo or 29.20 PLN annual):** Unlimited AI prompts, analytics, best times to post, competitor analysis, API access.
*   **The Vibe:** Packs every possible feature into a low price point.
*   **Limitation:** The UI can feel "cluttered." Known for occasional API glitches. Pricing scales per additional social account.

### 3. Buffer (The Solopreneur's Choice)
*   **Website:** [buffer.com](https://buffer.com)
*   **Pricing (USD - Per Channel):**
    *   **Free ($0):** Up to 3 channels, 10 scheduled posts/channel, 100 ideas, AI Assistant, API access (1 key, 3k requests/mo).
    *   **Essentials ($6/mo or $5/mo annual):** Unlimited scheduled posts, advanced analytics, hashtag manager, first comment scheduling, API access (3 keys, 7.5k requests/mo).
    *   **Team ($12/mo or $10/mo annual):** Unlimited team members, content approval workflows, API access (5 keys, 15k requests/mo).
*   **The Vibe:** Famously clean and distraction-free.
*   **Limitation:** They treat video as just "another file type." Lacks advanced creator features like AI beat-syncing. Pricing scales linearly per channel.

### 4. VistaSocial (The Agency Powerhouse)
*   **Website:** [vistasocial.com](https://vistasocial.com)
*   **Pricing (USD):**
    *   **Standard ($39/mo):** 8 profiles, 1 user.
    *   **Professional ($79/mo):** 15 profiles, 3 users.
    *   **Advanced ($149/mo):** 30 profiles, 6 users.
*   **The Vibe:** Sophisticated "Unified Inbox" and review management.
*   **Limitation:** Steep learning curve; the interface is dense with professional-grade tools.

### 5. SocialBee (The Evergreen Specialist)
*   **Website:** [socialbee.com](https://socialbee.com)
*   **Pricing (USD):**
    *   **Bootstrap ($29/mo or $14.50/mo annual):** 5 profiles, 1 user, 1 workspace, 10 content categories/sources, unlimited AI generation.
    *   **Accelerate ($49/mo or $24.50/mo annual):** 10 profiles, 1 user, 50 content categories, 30 sources, hashtag organizer, bulk editor.
    *   **Pro ($99/mo or $49.50/mo annual):** 25 profiles, 3 users, 5 workspaces, unlimited categories/sources, analytics reports.
    *   **Agency ($179 - $449/mo):** 50 to 150 profiles, 5 users/workspace, 10-30 workspaces.
*   **The Vibe:** Focuses on "Categorized" content recycling.
*   **Limitation:** No unified engagement inbox for responding to comments. (Current 2026 promo: 50% off for 1 year).

### 6. Ocoya (The AI-First Marketer)
*   **Website:** [ocoya.com](https://ocoya.com)
*   **Pricing (USD):**
    *   **Bronze ($19/mo or $15/mo annual):** 5 profiles, 1 user, 1 workspace, 100 AI credits.
    *   **Silver ($49/mo or $39/mo annual):** 20 profiles, 5 users, 5 workspaces, 500 AI credits.
    *   **Gold ($99/mo or $79/mo annual):** 50 profiles, 20 users, 20 workspaces, 1,500 AI credits.
    *   **Diamond ($199/mo or $159/mo annual):** 150 profiles, 50 users, unlimited workspaces, unlimited credits.
*   **The Vibe:** Built-in AI Graphic Design and copy generation.
*   **Limitation:** Known for platform stability issues during API updates.

### 7. ShortSync (The Video Specialist)
*   **Website:** [shortsync.app](https://shortsync.app)
*   **Pricing (EUR):**
    *   **Free (€0):** 5 uploads per month, bulk uploading, scheduling.
    *   **Creator (€14.99/mo or €11.99/mo annual):** Unlimited uploads, 1 workspace.
    *   **Team (€49.99/mo or €39.99/mo annual):** Unlimited uploads, 10 workspaces, team collaboration.
*   **The Vibe:** 100% focused on the vertical video movement.
*   **Limitation:** No text/image support; strictly for video specialists.

### 8. Directly (The Native Alternative)
*   **Website:** [directly.social](https://directly.social)
*   **Pricing:**
    *   **Free (Local Core):** 1-2 connections, local vault, manual posting.
    *   **Managed ($19/mo):** Hosted dashboard, automatic updates.
    *   **Agency ($49/mo):** Multi-brand management, team roles.
*   **The Vibe:** Privacy-first, developer-friendly, and subscription-free for self-hosters.
*   **Limitation:** Requires custom setup (Developer API credentials).

## ⚡ The "Magic Feature" Gap Analysis

### Feature 1: Trending Music Swapping
*   **The Problem:** Most users want to "use trending audio" to win the algorithm.
*   **The Competitors:** NO tool does this automatically via API. Most "auto-posters" burn the music into the video, which means the platform sees it as "Original Audio" and doesn't give you the "Trending" algorithmic boost.
*   **Directly Edge:** We can build a **"Sound-Check"** utility that scans TikTok trends and suggests the right sound to "pick" in the native app after our portal uploads the file.

### Feature 2: Platform "Vibe" Rewriting
*   **The Problem:** Cross-posting a "chaotic" TikTok caption to LinkedIn looks unprofessional. 
*   **The Competitors:** VistaSocial and Ocoya have "Tone" switches, but you have to manually trigger them for every post. 
*   **Directly Edge:** We can build a **"Global Vibe Sync"** that automatically produces three variants (Professional, Casual, Hype) for every upload without you clicking a single button.

## 🚀 The Directly Strategy
We will position as the **Native alternative** to these SaaS tools. While they focus on "Managing Channels," we focus on **"Mastering the Creator's Content."** 

### Strategic Pillars:
1.  **Direct API (No Middleware):** Unlike tools that route through their cloud, we push directly from your device.
2.  **Privacy-First:** Your media stays on your disk/vault.
3.  **Self-Owned:** One-time setup, zero monthly subscription fees per channel.

## 💎 The "Must-Have" Feature Wishlist
To beat the incumbents, Directly will prioritize these high-value workflow features:

1.  **Unified Engagement Inbox:** Reply to comments from all platforms via direct local API (Privacy-First).
2.  **Content Vault & Recycling:** Automated "Evergreen" post buckets to keep the feed active during creative droughts.
3.  **Pulse Analytics:** A high-aesthetic, unified dashboard for "Total Reach" across the entire social footprint.
4.  **AI Idea-to-Asset:** Built-in generators for background music, overlays, and platform-specific thumbnail variants.
5.  **One-Click "Global Vibe Sync":** Automatic translation and tone-shifting for different platform cultures (Hype for TT, Professional for LI).
