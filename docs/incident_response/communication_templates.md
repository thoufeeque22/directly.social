# Communication Templates

These templates are designed to streamline communication during an incident, ensuring users receive clear, consistent, and timely updates.

## Status Page / Social Media Updates

### 1. Investigating (Initial Response)

**Subject/Status:** Investigating: [Brief description of the issue]

**Message:** We are currently investigating reports of [brief description of the issue, e.g., "elevated error rates on login" or "service unavailability"]. We are working to identify the root cause and will provide an update shortly. Thank you for your patience.

### 2. Identified (Issue Found)

**Subject/Status:** Identified: [Brief description of the issue]

**Message:** We have identified the cause of [brief description of the issue] and are actively working on a fix. We will provide another update in [Timeframe, e.g., 30 minutes] or as soon as the issue is resolved.

### 3. Monitoring (Fix Applied)

**Subject/Status:** Monitoring: [Brief description of the issue]

**Message:** We have implemented a fix for [brief description of the issue] and are currently monitoring the results. Service should be returning to normal. We will confirm full resolution shortly.

### 4. Resolved

**Subject/Status:** Resolved: [Brief description of the issue]

**Message:** The issue causing [brief description of the issue] has been fully resolved. We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate your patience. 

*(Optional for P1/P2: A detailed post-mortem will be shared in the coming days.)*

## Direct Email to Affected Users (For targeted issues)

**Subject:** Update regarding your account / [Feature Name]

**Message:**
Hi [User Name/There],

We wanted to reach out regarding an issue you may have experienced with [Feature Name/Service]. Between [Start Time] and [End Time], we experienced [brief description of the issue].

Our team has identified the cause and applied a fix. The service is now fully operational.

[Optional: If data was affected, explain clearly what happened and what steps they need to take, if any.]

We sincerely apologize for the disruption. If you continue to experience any issues, please reply to this email, and I will assist you directly.

Best regards,
[Your Name/The directly.social Team]
