# Saving Time with Metadata Snippets

If you find yourself retyping the same hashtags, bio links, or disclaimers for every post, **Metadata Snippets** are for you. Snippets allow you to save reusable blocks of text and inject them into your titles or descriptions with a single click.

## How it Works

1.  **Create a Snippet:** Go to **Settings > Snippets** in your dashboard.
2.  **Save Your Text:** Give your snippet a label (e.g., "Standard Hashtags" or "Bio Link") and enter the text you want to reuse.
3.  **Inject While Drafting:** When you are uploading a new video in the **Media** tab, click the **Snippet Icon** (or "Insert Snippet" button) next to the title or description field.
4.  **Select & Insert:** Choose your snippet from the list, and it will be instantly appended to your text.

## Popular Use Cases

-   **Hashtag Clouds:** Save different sets of viral hashtags for specific niches (e.g., #cooking, #tech, #travel).
-   **Call to Actions:** "Click the link in my bio for the full recipe!"
-   **Disclosures:** Add mandatory ad disclosures or affiliate links consistently.
-   **Social Handles:** Quickly add your @handles for other platforms.

## Pro Tip: Placeholders
You can use snippets anywhere in your text. If you want to insert a snippet in the middle of a sentence, simply place your cursor where you want it before clicking the insert button.
