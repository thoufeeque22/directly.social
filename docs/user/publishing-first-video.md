# Publishing Your First Video

Ready to start publishing? Directly Social makes it easy to manage your videos and push them to all your social channels with a single click.

## Step 1: Upload to Your Vault
1. Navigate to the **Media** tab.
2. Click **Upload Video** (or drag and drop your file).
3. Your video will be securely uploaded directly to your storage vault (local or cloud).

## Step 2: Vibe Sync (AI Captions)
1. Once uploaded, click on the video to open the **Metadata Editor**.
2. Enter your "Core Concept" or a rough draft of your caption.
3. Use the **Vibe Sync** button to instantly generate platform-specific versions of your caption.
   - *Example:* A professional tone for YouTube, but a fast-paced, high-energy tone for TikTok.
4. Save your metadata.

## Step 3: Distribution & Scheduling
1. In the video details view, select the platforms you want to publish to (TikTok, YouTube, IG, FB).
2. Choose your publishing mode:
   - **Publish Now:** Sends the video immediately.
   - **Schedule:** Choose a future date and time from the calendar.
3. Click **Distribute**.

## Step 4: Tracking Success
1. Go to the **Activity** tab to see the real-time status of your posts.
2. Once a post is "Published," you can view its permalink directly or check the analytics to see how it's performing.

**Pro Tip:** Use **Metadata Snippets** in Settings to save your most common hashtags and disclaimers, so you never have to type them again!
