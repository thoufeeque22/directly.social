# AI Content Enhancement & Vibe Sync

directly.social helps you polish your content to match the "vibe" of different platforms. Whether you need to turn a serious caption into a viral TikTok hook or generate trending hashtags, our AI tools have you covered.

## Prerequisites
To use these features, you must first connect your own AI account (e.g., OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic, or Groq) in **Settings > AI Configuration**. See our [Power Users AI Guide](/docs/dev/ai-byok-guide) for help getting your API keys.

## Core AI Features

### 1. Vibe Sync (Tone Shifting)
Every platform has a different "language." What works on YouTube often fails on TikTok. 
-   **How to use:** After entering a draft description, click the **Vibe Sync** button.
-   **Options:** Choose from vibes like "Viral Hook," "Professional," "Informative," or "Minimalist."
-   **Result:** The AI will rewrite your caption to better fit that specific platform's algorithm and culture.

### 2. Hashtag Generator
Struggling to find the right tags?
-   **How to use:** Click the **Generate Hashtags** button next to your description.
-   **Result:** The AI analyzes your title and description to suggest 5-10 high-reach hashtags.

### 3. Title Polishing
Turn boring titles into click-worthy headlines.
-   **How to use:** Click the **Magic Wand** icon next to the title field.
-   **Result:** The AI provides 3 alternative titles designed to improve your click-through rate (CTR).

## Privacy Note
Because you use your own API keys, your content is sent directly to the AI provider (e.g., OpenAI). directly.social does not store your prompts or the generated content on our servers.
