# Getting Started: How to Login

Welcome to directly.social! The first step to native publishing is securely logging into your dashboard.

## Supported Login Providers

directly.social allows you to log in using your existing social media or professional accounts. This makes setup faster by automatically integrating your primary profile. Currently supported providers include:

-   **Google:** Recommended for most users (integrates YouTube).
-   **TikTok:** Use your TikTok creator account.
-   **Facebook / Meta:** Use your Facebook or Instagram Business credentials.

## How to Login

1.  Navigate to the [Login Page](/login).
2.  Select your preferred provider from the list.
3.  You will be redirected to the provider's official secure login page.
4.  Authorize directly.social to access your basic profile information.
5.  Once authorized, you will be redirected back to your **Dashboard**.

## ⚠️ Important: Consistency is Key

To ensure you always access the same data and settings, **we strongly recommend always using the same login method.**

-   **Account Duplication:** If you log in with Google today and TikTok tomorrow, directly.social may treat these as two separate accounts. Your videos, snippets, and settings will not be shared between them.
-   **Primary Method:** We recommend using **Google Login** as your primary method, as it is highly stable and widely supported across all our features.

> **Privacy Note:** We never see or store your passwords. All authentication is handled directly by your chosen provider through secure industry-standard protocols.
